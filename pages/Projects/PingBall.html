Ping Ball

A basic 2d ping pong game made using the unity engine.

You can choose between a game for two players and a singleplayer against an ai opponent.
Multiplyer is played on a single screen, on one keyboard. Controlls are W and S for player 1, up and down arrow keys for player 2.

Upon selecting the singleplayer option you can choose the difficulty.
Easy, medium and hard difficulties use a very simple algorithm that follows the position of the ball. Higher difficulty opponents are slightly faster and more precise at following the ball.
The impossible difficulty is more complex, where it predicts the trajectory of the ball using Unity's physics and rigid body systems.

Player wins on reaching 5 points.
