Tic-Tac-Toe

A game of Tic-Tac-Toe for up to two players.

The whole game is played in a command prompt window, which means nothing is clickable and you have to submit your action through writing the index of your chosen action. In this case we can write "1" *Enter* for new game or "2" *Enter* for a quick tutorial.


On choosing "New Game" you can put in your player names. You can choose an AI opponent by naming any of the players AI. This way you can even pit two AIs against each other and watch them play a very quick game of TicTacToe.

When a player wins, you are greeted with a victory message
and the main menu where you can choose to play again or exit the game.
